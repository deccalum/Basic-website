Microcontrollers & PCB Projects

How and Why

Throughout the years you tend to end up with a lot of old electronics and the likes. I've always been interested in how things work and how to make them work better or differently. So I started learning about microcontrollers and PCB design. I'm slowly learning working with Arduino/raspberry PI microcontrollers and PCB's to create my own custom electronics.

Realising the Complexity

As I started learning more about electronics and microcontrollers, I quickly realized that it's a complex field with a steep learning curve. There are so many different components, protocols, and design considerations to take into account. But I'm determined to keep learning and improving my skills.

Future Projects

In the future, I plan to work on more advanced projects that combine multiple microcontrollers and PCB's. I'm interested in exploring IoT (Internet of Things) applications, home automation, and wearable technology. I'm also looking forward to collaborating with other enthusiasts and sharing my knowledge with the community.
